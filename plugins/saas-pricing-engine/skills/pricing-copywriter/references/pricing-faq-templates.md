## Billing & Payment

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
A: Yes. You can switch to annual billing at any time and receive a prorated credit for your current billing period. Switching from annual to monthly takes effect at your next renewal.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice with NET-30 terms.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: We offer a [X]-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within [X] days of your first payment for a full refund. Annual plans are prorated if cancelled after the guarantee period.

**Q: Will my price change after I sign up?**
A: Your price is locked in for the duration of your billing period. We give at least 30 days notice before any pricing changes, and existing customers are grandfathered at their current rate for [X months/until next renewal].

## Upgrades & Downgrades

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**
A: Absolutely. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you're only charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Your data is never deleted. If you downgrade to a tier with lower limits, you'll retain read-only access to everything beyond the new tier's limits. You can export it anytime, and upgrading again restores full access.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fees for any self-serve plan. Enterprise plans with custom onboarding may include a one-time implementation fee — we'll discuss this during the sales process.
